About us

Who we are?

We aim to reduce pollution and promote sustainable energy sources. We create awareness about the importance of protecting ecosystems and their habitats. Our mission is to educate and inspire individuals and communities to take action towards a greener future. We believe that by working together, we can make a significant impact on the environment and ensure a better world for future generations.

projects

What we wish to do?

1. Install windmills at sea shore areas, hilly areas. Collect the wind energy and use it for generating electricity.

2. Installation of solar panels at barren lands, deserts to gather the direct solar rays falling on these panels to utilize for generation of heat energy as well as electricity.

3. Converting the waste organic waste and animal feed into biogas. The waste generated at the end can be used as manure for the plants and biogas can be used as fuel in kitchens.
